Matt's easy Mocha

For the poor man on the go who likes a little chocolate with his coffe!

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee
  • Hot chocolate
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Put one teaspoon of coffe into your cup
  2. Add one teaspoon of hot chocolate
  3. Add hot water and stir well
  4. Add milk to taste and enjoy
