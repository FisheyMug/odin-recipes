Pizza

Who doesn't like pizza!?

For that unbeatable flavour and cheesy goodness who can resist a simple pizza!

Follow along below for our simple pizza recipe!

Ingredients

  • Base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Any desired topping (you do you!)

Directions

  1. Take your pre mand pizza base and lather it in Tomato sauce
  2. Add your cheese and desired toppings
  3. Place in pre-heated oven on high for aproximately 10 mins or until cheese is melted and golden!
  4. Enjoy!
